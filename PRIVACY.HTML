Privacybeleid

Who we are
Our website address is: https://BootKeukens.nl

Comments

When visitors leave comments on the site, we collect the data shown in the comments form, and also the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent string to help spam detection.

An anonymized string created from your email address (also called a hash) may be provided to the Gravatar service to see if you are using it. The Gravatar service privacy policy is available here: https://automattic.com/privacy/. After approval of your comment, your profile picture is visible to the public in the context of your comment.

Media

If you upload images to the website, avoid uploading images with embedded location data (EXIF GPS). Visitors can download and extract any location data from images.

Cookies

If you leave a comment, you may opt-in to saving your name, email, and website in cookies. These last for one year.

If you visit our login page, a temporary cookie is set to determine if your browser accepts cookies.

Login cookies last two days; screen option cookies last a year. Selecting "Remember Me" keeps login for two weeks.

Embedded Content

Articles may include embedded content such as videos, images, or articles. These services may collect data, use cookies, or track your interaction.

Who we share your data with

If you request a password reset, your IP address will be included in the reset email.

How long we retain data

Comments and metadata are stored indefinitely. Registered users can edit or delete their personal information.

Your rights

You can request an exported file of your data or request deletion. This excludes data kept for legal or security reasons.

Where data is sent

Visitor comments may be checked through an automated spam detection service.
